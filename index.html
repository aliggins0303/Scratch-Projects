Variables store information.

For example, you can create a variable to keep track of everytime your character earns a point. You would probably name this variable "score".

You can create all kinds of variables for tracking progress in your game, such as score, # of character lives, etc. To make a variable, click on the Data tab in scratch and select "Make a Variable".

Cloning allows a sprite to duplicate itself multiple times throughout your scratch game or project. Clones of a sprite will be the same as the original sprite. Clones inherit the parent's scripts, costumes, sounds and properties; but they can still be modified.

To clone your sprite, click on the Controls tab in scratch.
